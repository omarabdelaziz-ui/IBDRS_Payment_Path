Tier System — Payment Path Logic

Not all entities can pay off debt in 9 months. The IBDRS assigns realistic payment paths based on debt size:
TIER A >$100K — 12-month plan, pay 40% of debt (M1:10%, then ~2.7%/mo). Remaining rolls to next MC term. Incentive: up to 4%.
TIER B $25K-$100K — 12-month plan, pay 60% of debt (M1:15%, then ~4.1%/mo). Incentive: up to 6%.
TIER C $10K-$25K — 9-month plan, pay 80% of debt (M1:20%, then ~7.5%/mo). Incentive: up to 8%.
TIER D $3K-$10K — 6-month plan, pay 100% with IBDRS milestones (M1:30%, M2:50%, M3:75%, settle by M3). Incentive: up to 10%.
TIER E <$3K — Full settlement in 1-3 months. Pay 100% upfront or in 2 installments. Incentive: up to 10%.
